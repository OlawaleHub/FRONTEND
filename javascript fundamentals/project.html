about

Regiscan is a free system that aims to solve the problem of long
queues and writing of attendance on paper. With Regiscan,
attendees of an events, seminar or class can simply scan the QR
code of their host and input their data. Morever, the time and
date can be very helpful for employers to keep track of the time
their employees check in.
